A Journal For Your Prices

Shop Smart, Save Money

Price Log is an app that helps you keep track of prices, for the things you buy most often, at the stores where you normally shop.

"A dollar saved is a dollar earned!"

Prices change from store to store and day to day. The differences are often small and hard to notice, but it all adds up!

Price Logs have long been useful tools for smart shoppers to find deals and save money. Now you can do this right on your phone. You log the things you buy, their prices, and where you buy them. Price Log automatically compares the prices and sorts out which store has the best price for each item.

You'll have a personalized guide to help you find deals and plan your next shopping trip.

A Practical Example

A dozen eggs cost $1.48 at one store and $2.39 at another. The difference is small. "It's less than a dollar," you've told yourself before, if you noticed at all. But lets say your household goes through about a dozen eggs a week. That adds up to around $4 saved a month, or $50 a year.

That saved money can add up to a free credit card bill, movie date, video game...

It's free money!

Multiply this scenario by ten or so similar items that you buy on a regular basis (food, drinks, hygiene, household supplies, etc.) and now that $50 is more like $500. That's a free vacation, car or house loan payment... Did you know you can cut years off a loan term with just one extra payment a year?

Saving money takes a little extra effort, but Price Log makes it easy!

How it Works

  • Go shopping!
  • Pay attention to prices, and log them in the app.
  • Next time, try a different store, and log those prices too.
  • Watch as Price Log sorts the prices and finds the deals.
  • Use the included Shopping List feature, to plan your next trip.
  • Save money!
